Our Mission

To Give You The Power.

Mobile plans in Canada are broken. Hidden fees, confusing promos, and plans that reward new customers while punishing loyal ones. Across the industry, plans are sold based on what earns the most — not what's best for you. Salespeople at many companies push phones and overpriced plans just to hit targets. That's why we built Switch My Plan — to bring transparency, unlock real savings, and put the power back where it belongs: In your hands.

Switch smart. Save better. Own your freedom.

What We Do

We're like Trivago for mobile plans — but better. Built for Canadians. Built for clarity.

Compare instantly: View real-time deals across major Canadian carriers—all in one place.

Switch effortlessly: For carriers like Bell and Virgin, we've built direct integrations so you can activate plans right from our site.

Redirect smartly: For others, we'll send you straight to their best available offers—no fluff, no up-selling.

We don't sell you plans. We help you find your plan.

How We're Different

Experience: We've been on the industry. We know the tricks—and we're using that knowledge to protect you.

No pressure, no pitch: We're not chasing quotas. We're not here to sell. We're here to serve. When you save — we win.

Built for trust: We partner with providers, but we work for the customer. That means transparent comparisons and clear recommendations, every time.

Our Vision

Right now, we're focused on helping people in Ontario find the best BYOD (Bring Your Own Device) plans. But this is just the start. Our goal is to become Canada's first fully online, commission-free mobile dealer. No stores. No salespeople. Just smart, simple service—where you can compare plans, get a phone on contract, and activate everything online. Your device gets shipped to your door or picked up from your nearest carrier store. Right now, we're doing this with no backing from carriers. We're not paid for what we do—we do it because we believe this industry needs change. One day, we'll partner directly with carriers as an affiliate or independent dealer. Until then, we'll keep proving there's a better way.
